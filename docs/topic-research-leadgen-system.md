# Topic Research & Lead Generation System

## Advanced Topic Research Framework

### 1. Pain Point Discovery System

#### Client Interview Process
**Monthly Client Calls (30 minutes each)**
- What marketing challenges keep you up at night?
- What tools are you struggling with?
- What metrics are you trying to improve?
- What's your biggest time waster?
- What would make your job easier?

**Documentation Template:**
```
Client: [Name/Company]
Industry: [Sector]
Company Size: [Employees/Revenue]
Primary Challenge: [Specific problem]
Secondary Issues: [List 3-5]
Current Solutions: [What's not working]
Budget Range: [If disclosed]

Content Interest: [What they want to learn]
```

**LinkedIn Group Monitoring**
- Marketing Automation Professionals
- B2B Marketing Leaders
- Revenue Operations Alliance

**Documentation:**

### 2. Lead Capture Workflows

#### LinkedIn Lead Capture

**Profile Optimization:**
```
Headline Formula: "I help [target audience] achieve [specific result] without [common pain point]"
About Section: Problem-solution-story framework with clear CTA
Featured Section: Lead magnets with compelling visuals
Contact Settings: Open to connection requests and messages
```

**Post CTA Strategy:**
- Soft CTA: "DM me for the template"
- Medium CTA: "Link in comments for full guide"
- Hard CTA: "Book a free strategy call"
- Community CTA: "Join our marketing leaders group"

**DM Sequence:**
```
Message 1 (Immediate): "Thanks for reaching out! Here's the [resource]. What specific challenge are you facing with [topic]?"

Message 2 (2 days later): "How did the [resource] work for you? I noticed [specific insight about their company/role]."

Message 3 (1 week later): "Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to discuss [specific opportunity]? No sales pitch - just want to help."
```

#### Reddit Lead Capture

**Comment Strategy:**
- Provide 80% of the solution publicly
- Offer the remaining 20% via PM
- Include social proof in responses
- Reference similar client situations

**PM Follow-up:**
```
Initial Response: "Happy to help! I have [specific resource] that walks through this exact situation. Used it with [similar company] and saw [specific result]. Want me to send it over?"

Follow-up: "Here's the [resource]. I also noticed [specific observation about their situation]. If you're open to it, I can share how we solved a similar challenge for [client type]."

Conversion: "We actually built a [tool/service] that automates this entire process. Happy to show you how it works if you're curious - no pressure though."
```

### 3. Lead Qualification System

#### Scoring Criteria

**Demographic Scoring:**
- Industry match (1-5 points)
- Company size fit (1-5 points)
- Role/title alignment (1-5 points)
- Geographic location (1-3 points)

**Behavioral Scoring:**
- Content engagement level (1-5 points)
- Lead magnet downloads (2-8 points)
- Website visits (1-5 points)
- Email opens/clicks (1-5 points)
- Social media interaction (1-3 points)

**Qualification Questions:**
```
1. What's your biggest marketing challenge right now?
2. What tools are you currently using?
3. What's your monthly marketing budget?
4. Who makes the final decision on marketing tools/services?
5. What's your timeline for implementing a solution?
```

#### Nurture Sequences

**High-Intent Leads (Score 15+):**
- Immediate personal outreach
- Book strategy call within 48 hours
- Send relevant case studies
- Offer free audit/consultation

**Medium-Intent Leads (Score 8-14):**
- Enter 30-day email sequence
- Share valuable resources weekly
- Invite to webinars/events
- Soft pitch after value delivery

**Low-Intent Leads (Score 1-7):**
- Monthly newsletter subscription
- Automated email sequence (90 days)
- Social media engagement
- Quarterly check-in calls
