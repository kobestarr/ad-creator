# GHL Communities Research (GoHighLevel)

**Research Date:** 2026-02-01  
**Status:** Complete

---

## Overview

GoHighLevel (GHL) is an all-in-one marketing platform that includes a Communities feature for building and monetizing online communities.

---

## Key Features

### Communities
- **Unlimited community spaces** - Create as many as needed
- **Standalone or course-connected** - Communities can exist independently or be linked to courses
- **Engagement tools** - Posts, comments, direct messaging
- **Member management** - Track engagement, activity

### Course Creation
- **Unlimited courses** - Video, audio, file uploads
- **Payment models** - One-time, payment plans, subscriptions
- **Community integration** - Students can discuss in linked community

### Membership Sites
- **Subscription-based** - Regular recurring revenue
- **Regular updates** - Keep members engaged
- **Multiple tiers** - Different access levels
