# Twine.net Freelance Platform Research

**Research Date:** 2026-02-01  
**Status:** Complete

---

## Overview

Twine.net is a marketplace connecting businesses with skilled freelancers in tech, creative, and marketing. It emphasizes quality work and legitimate opportunities.

---

## Pricing Structure

### For Clients

| Plan | Cost | Features |
|------|------|----------|
| Standard | Free | Post projects, hire freelancers, 5-10% service fee |
| Business | $139.99/project | Dedicated Customer Success rep, personalized vetting, consolidated billing |

**Service Fee (Standard):**
- 10% on payments up to $10,000
- 5% on payments over $10,000

### For Freelancers

| Plan | Cost | Features |
|------|------|----------|
| Free | $0 | 15 job applications/month, portfolio build |
| Pro | $7.49/month | Unlimited applications, Pro badge, see who's viewed portfolio |

---

## How It Works

### For Freelancers
1. Create profile with portfolio
2. Browse matching projects
3. Apply with proposal
4. Client reviews and hires
5. Deliver work via Twine Vault (secure payment)

### Key Features
- Portfolio-first discovery
- Secure payment protection
- 7-day refund policy if freelancer fails
- Consolidated billing for enterprise
- Global network (500K+ freelancers)

## Best Practices (From 2026 Guide)

### Profile Optimization
- Write like a landing page
- Who you help + outcomes delivered + proof (portfolio, testimonials)

### Productize Your Offer
Create one clearly scoped "starter" offer:
- Brand identity audit
- 5-asset ad creative bundle
- Landing page teardown

### Track Effective Hourly Rate
Include:
- Platform fees (10% then 5%)
- Unpaid admin time
- Revisions
- Tools/subscriptions
- Taxes
