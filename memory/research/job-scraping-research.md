## Overview

## Key Methods

### 1. Job Board Monitoring
**Targets:**
- Upwork (filtered by skills)
- LinkedIn Jobs (Easy Apply)
- Indeed
- PeoplePerHour
- Toptal (for senior roles)

**Tools:**
- Apify actors (job board scrapers)
- Custom Python scrapers
- CaptainData for enrichment

### 2. Outreach Sequence
**Step 1:** Scrape matching jobs  
**Step 2:** Enrich with company data (CaptainData)  
**Step 3:** Find hiring manager contact (Hunter.io, Apollo.io)  
**Step 4:** Personalize outreach email  
**Step 5:** Send follow-up sequence  

### 3. Personalization Template
```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is looking for [role] on [platform]. 
[Relevant insight about their company recent news/work]

We help companies like [similar company] achieve [result].
[1-sentence case study]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to see if we could help?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Reddit Insights (Nick Saraev Method)

Key learnings from Reddit contributors:

1. **Niche down** - Don't be generalist
2. **Show, don't tell** - Include case studies
3. **Speed matters** - Apply within 24 hours of posting
4. **Follow up** - 70% of replies come from follow-ups
5. **Track everything** - Spreadsheet CRM is essential
