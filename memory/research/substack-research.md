**Research Date:** 2026-02-01

## Overview

Substack is a newsletter platform enabling paid subscriptions. Creators can earn 6+ figures by building loyal audiences and offering exclusive content.

---

## Monetization Models

### 1. Paid Subscriptions
**How it works:**
- Free tier for email list building
- Paid tier ($5-15/month or $50-150/year) for exclusive content
- Subscribers pay for ongoing access to your unique worldview

**Example earners:**
- Emily Atkin (Heated): Six-figure climate newsletter
- Many creators: $5K-50K/month

### 2. Sponsorships
**How it works:**
- Brand sponsorships in newsletter
- Substack has an Ad Network for matching
- Can also negotiate directly

**Rates:**
- Small lists (1K+): $50-200/sponsor
- Large lists (10K+): $500-5,000+/sponsor

### 3. Affiliate Marketing
**How it works:**
- Recommend tools/services
- Earn commission on referrals
- Examples: Software, courses, books

### 4. Multiple Products
**Combine:**
- Paid newsletter
- Digital products (courses, ebooks)
- Consulting/coaching
- Community access

## Content Strategy for Repurposing

### Repurposing Flow
```
One Content Piece → Multiple Formats → Multiple Platforms

Example: Podcast Episode
    → Written summary (Substack)
    → Key takeaways (LinkedIn)
    → Quote graphics (Instagram)
    → Thread (Twitter)
    → Video clips (YouTube Shorts)
```

### Substack-Specific Content
**Free tier:**
- Public posts visible to all
- Drive traffic from other platforms
- Build email list

**Paid tier:**
- Deep dives not published elsewhere
- Exclusive analysis
- Community access
- Early access to content

## Best Practices

### Growing Your List
1. **Cross-promote** - Link Substack from all other platforms
2. **Free value** - Give away enough to build trust
3. **Consistency** - Publish schedule (weekly minimum)
4. **Email signature** - Always include Substack link

### Converting to Paid
1. **Build relationship** - 3-6 months of free content first
2. **Offer exclusive** - What paid subscribers get that free don't
3. **Launch momentum** - Announce paid tier, offer founding member pricing
4. **Deliver consistently** - Paid subscribers expect value

### Substack vs. beehiiv
**Substack:**
- Simpler, no customization
- Built-in audience discovery
- Easier to start

**beehiiv:**
- More features, customization
- Growth tools (Boosts)
- Better for scaling
