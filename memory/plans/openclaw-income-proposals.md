## Executive Summary

Research across Perplexity, Reddit, YouTube, and industry sources reveals:

- **OpenClaw** (formerly Clawdbot/Moltbot) enables 24/7 proactive AI agents
- **AI automation** saves 1.5-2.5 hours/week per user (reduces cognitive load)
- **57% of US work hours** can be automated by AI agents (McKinsey)
- **AI automation agencies** generating $100K-$1M+ with recurring revenue
- Shift from execution to orchestration/judgment is the future
