## FINAL HOOK WRITING RULES

### ‚úÖ DO:
1. Lead with the outcome/benefit FIRST
2. Make it specific and measurable when possible
3. Create a gap or tension ("78% try, 26% succeed")
4. Use active, strong verbs (Eliminates, Drives, Delivers)
5. Keep it concise and scannable
6. Reference real problems executives face
7. Show you understand the "valley of death" / pain points

### ‚ùå DON'T:
1. Start with setup or context
2. Use vague benefits ("better performance")
3. Bury the value proposition
4. Use passive voice
5. Lead with "We help companies..." (talk about THEM first)
6. Use buzzwords without substance
7. Make it about your process before their outcome
