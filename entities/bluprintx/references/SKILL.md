## Content Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### Outcome-First Hooks (CRITICAL)

**ALWAYS lead with POSITIVE outcome/benefit FIRST:**

The hook must make readers instantly associate BluprintX with positive outcomes and want to employ us to achieve them.

✅ POSITIVE OUTCOME-FIRST HOOKS:
- "Scale AI pilots to production in weeks. 26% of companies already do this."
- "Move from AI experiment to measurable ROI. Here's the proven path."
- "Turn AI pilots into profit centers. The framework that works."
- "24/7 booking that runs itself. Zero admin. Here's how."
- "Cut content time by 64% without hiring."
- "Activate AI value in weeks, not months. This is how winning teams do it."

❌ NEGATIVE OR PROBLEM-FOCUSED HOOKS (DO NOT USE):
- "74% of AI pilots never make it to production..." (starts with failure)
- "Your AI experiments are probably failing..." (assumes failure)
- "The biggest AI lie? That more pilots = more progress." (cynical/negative)
- "Only 26% of AI pilots scale..." (focuses on the 74% who fail)

**Hook Formula:**
"[Specific Positive Outcome] That [Solves Problem]. [How/What/This is how]."

**The Positive Outcome Test:**
Before using a hook, ask: "Does this make someone think 'I want that outcome' or 'I want to avoid that problem'?"
- If "I want that outcome" = GOOD HOOK
- If "I want to avoid that problem" = REWRITE to focus on the positive outcome

### BluprintX Brand Voice

**Tone Characteristics:**
1. Direct & confident (no hedging)
2. Problem-aware (acknowledges real challenges)
3. Outcome-obsessed (specific metrics)
4. Anti-buzzword (cuts through hype)
5. Action-oriented (Activate, Accelerate, Deliver)
6. Enterprise-focused
7. Partnership posture
8. POSITIVE (lead with what's possible, not what fails)

**Language to Use:**
✅ Fast, Rapid, Clear, Focused, Real, Measurable, Proven, End-to-end
✅ Active voice, specific metrics, outcomes as nouns
✅ Hyphens for compound modifiers, commas for pauses

**Language to Avoid:**
❌ Innovative, cutting-edge, revolutionary, best-in-class
❌ Fluffy adjectives, passive voice, theory before value
❌ EM DASHES (—) - ABSOLUTELY FORBIDDEN. Use hyphens (-) or restructure sentences with commas.

### Platform-Specific Adaptations

**LinkedIn:**
- Optimal length: 800-1,000 characters
- Use line breaks generously
- Max 3 hashtags (or none)
- Links: 0 links OR 4+ links (or put link in first comment)
- First 2 lines determine everything
- Carousels: MAX 5 pages (teasers only, not comprehensive guides)

**Instagram:**
- Reels: 9:16 vertical (1080x1920)
- Grid: 1080x1080 or 1080x1350
- Stories: 1080x1920
- Caption should be concise
- Use relevant hashtags (5-10)
- Carousels: MAX 5 pages

**Twitter/X:**
- Threads: 5-15 tweets ideal
- 70-100 characters per tweet for quick reads
- Numbered tweets (1/, 2/, 3/)
- Final tweet: 2-3 hashtags
- Link in first and last tweet

### Visual Asset Specifications

**LinkedIn Images:**
- 1200 x 627 pixels (1.91:1) or 1200 x 1500 (better for mobile)
- Carousel/Document: PDF, up to 300 pages

**LinkedIn Video:**
- Format: MP4 with H.264 codec, AAC audio
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080) - DO NOT upload 4K
- Bitrate: 8,000-10,000 kbps (prevents pixelation)
- Frame Rate: 24-30 fps
- Duration: Under 60 seconds best
- Aspect Ratios: 4:5 (1080x1350) BEST for mobile

**Instagram:**
- Feed: 1080x1080, 1080x566, 1080x1350
- Reels: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Stories: 1080x1920

**Twitter/X:**
- Images: 1200 x 675 pixels (16:9)
- Videos: 1280x720 or 1920x1080
